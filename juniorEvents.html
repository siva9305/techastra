INDIVIDUAL EVENTS

GEO Catch

A unique blend of escape room treasure hunt ,solving techenical sublect quizzes to un cover hidden treasure and escape room.

VISION EXPO

Showcase your ingenuity at vision expo,a dynamic project exhibition for student

PICTO SCOPE

Participants will be presented with sets of pictures that convey a hidden word or phrase.

JUNIOR-THON

competition for young innovators. Over four hours, students will collaborate to solve given problem statements.

CHESS CHALLENGE

Participants will face off in timed matches, showcasing their tactical thinking and problem-solving abilities.

TALENT KNIGHT

Participants will draw topics from bowls containing English and Tamil prompts and have two minutes to narrate a captivating story.

Brain Out Quiz Challenge

Test your knowledge and quick thinking at the Brain Out Quiz Challenge, a thrilling virtual quiz competition.

ScrambledXGO

Participants will test their technical knowledge and teamwork skills as they race against time to rearrange logos correctly.
